Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026

GreyPile ("the App") is a hobby army tracker for tabletop miniature painters. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the App's functionality:

  • Email address — collected when you create an account, used solely for authentication and account recovery.
  • Hobby data — army names, unit names, painting states, notes, and notification preferences that you choose to enter. This data is stored to sync across your devices.
  • Purchase status — whether you have purchased the Pro upgrade. No payment details are stored by us.
  • Activity data — dates on which you add or update entries, used only to generate in-app statistics visible to you.

We do not collect your real name, location, device identifiers, or any sensitive personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To sync your hobby data across your devices.
  • To send you local push notifications (only if you explicitly enable them in the App).
  • To verify your Pro purchase status.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the App.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database and authentication provider. Your email and hobby data are stored on Supabase servers. Supabase is GDPR-compliant and stores data in the EU. Supabase Privacy Policy →
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management. Used to verify and restore Pro purchases. RevenueCat Privacy Policy →
  • Apple — App Store distribution and optional Sign in with Apple. If you use Sign in with Apple, Apple may share a name and email with us. Apple Privacy Policy →

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data (email, armies, miniatures) is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your account and all associated data.
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at a.caserini@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

GreyPile is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
